Research Interests

Object Detection

Improving YOLO architectures for industrial defect detection and underwater environments. Designed ODE-RepGFPN feature fusion, CPMSCA attention, and FW-IoU loss for small object and imbalanced data challenges.

Visual Language Models

Fine-tuning VLM backbones (Qwen3-VL) for domain-specific tasks. Building automated annotation pipelines that leverage pre-trained vision-language models for efficient data labeling.

RLHF & Model Alignment

Applying reinforcement learning with GRPO reward mechanisms for vision-language model alignment. Multi-stage training pipeline combining SFT with RLHF for improved decision accuracy.

Robotics & Autonomous Systems

Developing intelligent robots for logistics, underwater cleaning, and autonomous driving. Integrating perception, decision-making, and control on embedded platforms (STM32).
